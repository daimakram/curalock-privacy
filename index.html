CuraLock
Privacy Policy · Effective 6 June 2026 · Contact: daimakram@gmail.com

Curalock is a private, offline health-records app for caregivers. Privacy isn't a feature here — it's the whole point. Your records never leave your device.

What we collect

No personal or health information — ever. Curalock has no account, no server, and no sign-in. There is no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no AI processing of your data. Your health records are never collected, transmitted, sold, or shared.

The app makes no network connection at all during normal use. The only time it goes online is when you choose to buy or restore the one-time premium upgrade — that purchase is handled by the App Store / Google Play and RevenueCat. That flow involves your purchase status, an anonymous identifier and basic device info — never your health records, and no payment details we can see (see "Purchases" below).

Where your data lives

Everything you enter — profiles, conditions, medicines, visits, symptoms, vitals, reports, vaccinations, surgeries, documents, expenses, photos and scans — is stored only in Curalock's private storage on your device. Attached photos/PDFs are copied into the app's own sandbox; we never upload them.

Things you choose to do (the only times data leaves the app)

These happen only when you tap them, through your phone's own apps — not through any server of ours:

  • Backups & exports — you can export your data (JSON, a .clbak archive, or a PDF summary) and save or send it via your phone's share sheet (e.g. to your own Google Drive, Files, or a message). Where it goes is entirely your choice.
  • Calls — tapping a phone number opens your dialer.
  • Support — contacting us via WhatsApp or email sends only your app version and device model (e.g. "Curalock 1.0.0 · Redmi Note 12") so we can help — never your health data. (We ask you not to include personal health details in support messages.)

Permissions

Each permission is used only on your device, for the stated purpose:

  • Camera / Photos — to attach a photo or scan to a record.
  • Notifications — to remind you about medicines, refills and follow-ups (scheduled locally; no push server).
  • Biometrics / Face ID — for the optional in-app lock; it only checks against your phone, nothing is transmitted.

Purchases

The one-time premium upgrade is processed through the Apple App Store / Google Play using RevenueCat, a third-party purchase-management service that verifies and restores your purchase. When you buy or restore the upgrade, RevenueCat receives your purchase status, an anonymous, app-generated identifier, and basic technical details (such as your device platform, app version and country) needed to complete the transaction. It never receives your name, your payment-card details, or any of your health records, and this data is not used for advertising or tracking. You can read how they handle it in RevenueCat's privacy policy. If you never buy the upgrade, none of this happens.

Children & sensitive data

Curalock is intended for users aged 13 and over. The app has no account and collects no personal information from any user — including children — beyond the optional purchase data described under "Purchases" (processed by Google Play / the App Store and RevenueCat). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Curalock may hold sensitive health information about the people you care for, which may include children. That information is entered by an adult caregiver, stays on the device, and is never sent anywhere — it's protected by your phone's own security (and Curalock's optional passcode/biometric lock). Keep a backup in a place you control.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update it here with a new effective date.
